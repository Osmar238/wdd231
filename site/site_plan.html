Site Name

SmartPlate

Reason: This name suggests intelligence ("Smart") in making dietary choices and refers directly to the act of eating ("Plate"). It is catchy, memorable, and clearly communicates the app's focus on healthy eating solutions.

Optional domain: smart-plate.app

Site Purpose

The purpose of SmartPlate is to solve the daily dilemma of "what to cook" while promoting a healthy lifestyle. The application allows users to find nutritious recipes based on ingredients they currently have in their pantry. It aims to reduce food waste and make healthy cooking accessible and straightforward by providing clear nutritional information and preparation steps.

Scenarios

Here are two common questions visitors might ask:

  • Scenario 1: "I have chicken breast, spinach, and rice in my kitchen, but I don't know how to combine them. What is a healthy, high-protein dinner I can make with these ingredients right now?"
  • Scenario 2: "I am looking for a dessert recipe that is gluten-free and takes less than 30 minutes to prepare. Where can I find options that fit these specific dietary restrictions?"

Background
  • Primary (#4CAF50): Used for the main header, footer, and primary call-to-action buttons. It represents freshness and health.
  • Secondary (#FF5722): Used for accents, highlights on hover, and important alerts (like "hot" recipes). It stimulates appetite and adds energy.
  • Text (#333333): Used for all body text to ensure high readability against the light background.
  • Background (#F9F9F9): A soft off-white used for the page background to reduce eye strain compared to pure white.

Typography

Headings: Roboto

The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog. (Used for h1, h2, h3)

Body Text: Open Sans

The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog. (Used for paragraphs, lists, and general content).

Rationale: Roboto provides a modern, geometric structure for headings that commands attention, while Open Sans is neutral and friendly, offering excellent readability for recipe instructions and ingredient lists.
